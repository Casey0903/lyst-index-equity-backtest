# Company-Level Composite Scores

> Score = Σ (brand_revenue_weight × brand_composite_score)
> Brand score = 0.5×Level + 0.3×Trend + 0.2×Presence
> Weights = brand revenue / parent total revenue (don't sum to 1.0 for conglomerates)
